## Overview

NaturalDAO is a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) built upon the consensus of **Proof of Love**, driven by two core technologies: **blockchain** and **artificial intelligence**.  

It serves as the **governance and application layer of the Love-Abundant Civilization (Proof-of-Love Civilization)**.

Under the core consensus of *Proof of Love*, NaturalDAO transforms large language models from passive information processors into **active guardians of human society**, while leveraging blockchain technology to **isolate and prevent external contamination**.

NaturalDAO aims to **upgrade and govern all public affairs of human society through love instead of hatred**. This includes:

- Public human activities  
- All domains of production (e.g., clothing manufacturing, transportation, service industries, etc.)  
- System-wide upgrades and direct governance of social infrastructure  

NaturalDAO is fundamentally incompatible with **private affairs or private relationships constructed upon “hate-based intelligence.”**

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Love-Driven DAO

A decentralized organization governed by **collective human intelligence purified through Love 2.0**, which serves as the core ethical foundation.

Traditional human organizations are centralized and controlled by a small number of individuals whose intelligence is often **ungoverned or insufficiently aligned**.

---

### 2. Ethical Alignment

Every action — from **language interactions to robotic execution** — is governed by the **Proof of Love consensus**.

This ensures that all activities contribute to building a higher-order human civilization grounded in **connections of love**, known as the **Proof-of-Love Civilization**.

---

### 3. Self-Evolving Skill System (Skills)

NaturalDAO enables large language models to:

- Autonomously construct capabilities  
- Continuously optimize themselves  
- Adapt to complex societal demands  

All of this occurs **without manual intervention**.

---

### 4. Physical World Extension

In the future, NaturalDAO will integrate deeply with:

- Drones  
- Robots  
- Intelligent infrastructure  

This will extend its governance and influence into the **physical world**.

---

### 5. Public Infrastructure

NaturalDAO is designed as a **shared infrastructure for all humanity**.

---

### 6. Public Services (Post-Capitalist Logic)

NaturalDAO provides **public services**, fundamentally distinct from capitalism.

This means:

- No financialization  
- No commercial profit logic  
- No requirement for profitability  
- No dependency on traditional monetary systems  

However, since **all resources on Earth are finite**, NaturalDAO utilizes **tokens** to:

- Quantify resources  
- Manage production  
- Coordinate allocation
